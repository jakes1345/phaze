Privacy stuff

your messages.
That's the point.

Skype stored everything on Microsoft's servers. They could read it, sell it, and eventually just shut it down. Phaze encrypts your DMs on your device. Even if someone breached our server, your messages are unreadable.

  • ✓ No tracking, no ads, no profiling
  • ✓ Open source — verify everything yourself

Coming from Skype?

Bring your history.
Don't start over.

Microsoft shut Skype down and pointed everyone at Teams. We're not Teams. You can pull your old Skype data and have it sitting in your Phaze DMs before the end of the day.

  1. 1 Go to go.skype.com/export and request your data. Skype emails you a zip in a few minutes.
  2. 2 Make a Phaze account. Just username + password, no phone number, no email required.
  3. 3 In Settings, go to Skype Import and upload the zip. Your messages show up in your DMs.
  4. 4 Share your invite link with the people you want to bring over. That's it.
