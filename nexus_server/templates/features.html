What's actually in Phaze

No empty marketing. Just the features we actually ship today.

🔒

End-to-end encrypted DMs

Every direct message is encrypted on your device with NaCl (Curve25519 + XSalsa20-Poly1305). The server stores the ciphertext but cannot read it.

📞

Voice & video calls

WebRTC P2P calls with automatic TURN fallback so connections work even behind symmetric NAT and corporate firewalls.

🎙️

Voice messages

Tap to record on the web client, send a quick voice note when typing won't cut it. Inline playback right in the chat.

💾

Cross-device history

Sign in on a new browser or device and your encrypted chat history follows you — protected by a Recovery PIN only you know.

✨

Modern chat UX

Reactions, message edits, deletes, @mentions with autocomplete, pinned messages, in-chat search, ⌘K command palette, slash commands.

📎

File & image sharing

Send images, audio, video, PDFs, archives — up to 25MB each. URLs are encrypted in the message body so only the recipient can fetch.

🌗

Dark mode & multi-platform

First-class light and dark themes. Web, Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android — sign in on every device with one Recovery PIN.

🖥

Screen sharing

Share your screen in any call or voice channel. Present, collaborate, or just show your friends what you're working on.

🌐

Spaces

Public or private community servers with text and voice channels. @mentions, search, pinned messages, inline editing — like Discord but encrypted.

📸

Stories

Share photos and short videos that disappear after 24 hours. Your friends see them in a ring at the top of the chat list.

🔴

Livestreaming

Go live with your camera or share your screen to anyone on Phaze. Viewers join with one click — no accounts required to watch.

🖱️

Remote control

Share your screen and let a friend take control — encrypted, peer-to-peer, built right in. Like TeamViewer but no extra app needed.

🛠️

Self-hostable

Don't trust phazechat.world? Run your own Nexus server with one Docker command. Your community, your servers, your rules.

🚫

No ads, no tracking

No third-party analytics, no behavioral profiling, no data sold to anyone. You can delete your account and export every byte we have on you.
