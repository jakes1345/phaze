Phaze Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 28, 2026

Short version: Phaze is built around end-to-end encryption. Your messages are encrypted on your device before they ever reach our servers. We cannot read your private conversations. We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the service.

1. Who we are

Phaze ("we", "us", "our") is an independent encrypted messaging application operated by the Phaze project. The service is accessible at phazechat.world and through the Phaze mobile and desktop clients.

For privacy questions contact: privacy@phazechat.world

2. What data we collect

Account information

  • Username — required to identify you on the network.
  • Email address — used for account verification and password recovery only. Not shared with third parties and not used for marketing.
  • Hashed password — stored as a salted bcrypt hash. We never store or transmit your plaintext password.

Messages and calls

  • Direct messages are end-to-end encrypted using NaCl (libsodium) box encryption. The server relays ciphertext only — we cannot decrypt your messages.
  • Voice and video calls are negotiated peer-to-peer via WebRTC. Call audio and video are never stored or recorded by our servers.
  • Message metadata (sender, recipient, timestamp) is stored to support offline delivery and is purged after delivery or after 30 days, whichever comes first.

Files and media

  • Files you send as attachments are uploaded to our server temporarily to support delivery to offline recipients. Files are deleted automatically after 7 days.
  • Stories (photos/videos) you post are stored until you delete them or after 24 hours.

Device and connection information

  • Your IP address and device model string ("android/Model") are logged for fraud prevention and are retained for a maximum of 30 days.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token — used to deliver push notifications to your Android device. This token is provided by Google and subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Session tokens — used to keep you logged in. Stored only on your device and our server. You can invalidate all sessions by signing out.

Cryptographic public keys

  • Your NaCl public key is shared with contacts so they can encrypt messages to you. This is intentional and required for E2EE to work.
  • Key backups (optional): if you use the key backup feature, your private key is encrypted with a PIN on your device before upload. We store the ciphertext only and cannot access your private key.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not read, scan, or analyse the content of your encrypted messages.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not use your data to train AI or machine-learning models.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.).

4. Third-party services

Phaze uses a limited set of third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivers push notifications. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • TURN/STUN relay servers — used to establish peer-to-peer calls when direct connections are blocked by NAT. TURN servers see encrypted RTP packets; they cannot decrypt call content.

5. Data retention

  • Account data: retained for the lifetime of your account, or 90 days after deletion request.
  • Message metadata: 30 days after delivery.
  • File attachments: 7 days after upload.
  • Stories: 24 hours or until manually deleted.
  • Connection logs (IP, device): 30 days.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request that your account and associated data be deleted.
  • Correction — update your username or email.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent — delete your account at any time from Settings → Sign Out → Delete Account.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@phazechat.world. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher. Private messages use an additional layer of NaCl end-to-end encryption. Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes with a cost factor of 12. The server database is stored on encrypted storage.

8. Children

Phaze is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us at privacy@phazechat.world and we will delete the account.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Phaze after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
