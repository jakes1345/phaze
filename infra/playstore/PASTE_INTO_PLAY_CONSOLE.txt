FULL DESCRIPTION:
-----------------------------------------------------
Phaze is an end-to-end encrypted messenger built for privacy-first communication. Your messages are encrypted on your device using NaCl (libsodium) box encryption before they ever leave your phone — even we can't read them.

🔐 TRUE END-TO-END ENCRYPTION
• NaCl public-key encryption for all direct messages
• Cryptographic key fingerprints you can verify with contacts
• Optional encrypted key backup protected by a PIN only you know
• No ads. No tracking. No data mining.

💬 EVERYTHING YOU NEED
• 1-on-1 encrypted chat with file and voice message support
• Voice and video calls + screen sharing over WebRTC
• Spaces — group channels with a public Discover directory
• Stories — share photos and short videos with your contacts
• Themes — Dark, Light, and a classic "Skype 7" look (plus snowflakes)
• Two-factor authentication (TOTP) for extra account security
• QR code device linking — sign in on a new device without a password

📱 BUILT FOR ANDROID
• Clean, modern Material design
• Push notifications via Firebase so you never miss a message
• Works great on Android 8.0 and above
• Prefer your browser? Phaze also runs as a full web app at phazechat.world — on any phone, tablet, or computer

🌐 OPEN & INDEPENDENT
• Open-source on GitHub
• Self-hostable — run your own Phaze relay server
• No vendor lock-in; your data stays under your control

Phaze is currently in beta. Core features are fully functional. Feedback welcome via GitHub Issues.
